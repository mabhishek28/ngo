About Us

Bal Seva Sansthan is an NGO in India based in New Delhi.Wwe have worked extensively to reach out and rehabilitate differently able people from the underprivileged segment while focusing on alleviating disabilities and providing proper Physical, Social, and Economic Rehabilitation to the needy. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programs in health, education, rehabilitation, corrective surgeries, and helping aid distribution making us the best charity organization in India.
